# Initial Concept
A personal quantitative sports betting platform designed for learning, fun, and profit. The platform focuses on identifying +EV (Positive Expected Value) wagering opportunities by leveraging a multi-agent system, sharp signal detection, and sophisticated Bayesian modeling.

# Product Definition

## Vision
To create a high-performance, automated intelligence layer for sports betting that transforms raw market data and news into actionable, high-probability betting signals. The platform acts as a personal quant desk, continuously learning and refining its models to stay ahead of the market.

## Target Audience
- **The Developer/Bettor (You):** Primarily designed as a personal tool for fun, learning, and financial gain.
- **Quantitative Enthusiasts:** People interested in the intersection of data science, Bayesian statistics, and sports markets.

## Core Goals
- **Maximize Edge:** Identify +EV wagers by devigging sharp bookmaker odds (Pinnacle/Circa) and comparing them against retail markets.
- **Model Accuracy:** Continuously improve probability estimation through Bayesian updates and multi-agent cross-validation.
- **Profitability:** Successfully identify and track wagers that yield consistent long-term returns.
- **Learning & Exploration:** Experiment with various predictive architectures, including Bayesian models, Multi-Agent systems, and classical ML (Linear Regression, Random Forest).

## Key Features
- **Sharp Odds Aggregation:** Real-time collection and devigging of odds from sharp books to establish a "true" market price.
- **Expert Reasoning Agent:** An LLM-powered agent using sequential thinking to provide qualitative context and final recommendations.
- **Signal Detection:** Automated monitoring for Reverse Line Movement (RLM) and Steam Moves as indicators of sharp activity.
- **Multi-Agent Synergy:** A coordinated swarm of agents (Odds, Analysis, Twitter, Expert) working together to validate every pick.
- **Omnichannel Reporting:** Seamless automated updates to Notion, Google Sheets, and Telegram.
- **Advanced Predictive Modeling:** A modular core supporting Bayesian modeling and potential expansion into classical ML methods (Linear Regression, Random Forests).
- **Model Comparison Framework:** Rigorous evaluation system to compare different model architectures (e.g., Bayesian vs. Random Forest) using Brier Score, ROI, and CLV.

## Success Metrics
- **CLV (Closing Line Value) Beats:** Frequency of beating the closing line on sharp books.
- **ROI (Return on Investment):** Long-term profitability of recommended wagers.
- **Model Calibration:** Alignment between predicted probabilities and actual outcomes.
- **Automation Reliability:** 100% success rate in daily reporting and data syncing.
